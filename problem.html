Problem

Problem Statement

Planning trips can be overwhelming due to the sheer number of options available and the challenge of coordinating group travel. Despite the high demand for personalized travel planning tools, many existing solutions provide generic itineraries that fail to capture the unique interests and local experiences travelers seek.

Key Challenges

  • Choice Overload: Too many options can lead to decision fatigue.
  • Generic Itineraries: Lack of personalization results in missed local experiences.
  • Inflexible Plans: Rigid itineraries do not accommodate spontaneous changes.
  • Group Coordination: Difficulty in harmonizing the preferences of multiple travelers.
